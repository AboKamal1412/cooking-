Classic Chicken Curry

This flavorful chicken curry is a comforting dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a spiced tomato and onion gravy. It's perfect served with rice or naan and makes a satisfying meal for any day of the week.

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 35 minutes
Total Time 50 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

35:00

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and cayenne. Cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add chicken pieces and stir to coat in the spices. Cook for 5–7 minutes, until lightly browned.
  5. Pour in crushed tomatoes and salt. Cover and simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in yogurt or coconut milk and simmer uncovered for another 10 minutes until the curry thickens and chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.

Chef's Tips

  • For deeper flavor, marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour before cooking.
  • Adjust spice level by increasing or decreasing cayenne pepper.
  • Add vegetables like peas or spinach for extra nutrition.
  • Use full-fat yogurt for a creamier texture, or coconut milk for a dairy-free version.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this chicken curry with:

  • Steamed basmati rice or jeera rice
  • Garlic or butter naan
  • Raita or cucumber yogurt salad
  • Mango chutney or pickles
