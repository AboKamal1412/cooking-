Lemon Garlic Roast Chicken

This lemon garlic roast chicken is juicy, tender, and bursting with bright citrus flavor. Perfectly seasoned and roasted until golden brown, it makes a stunning centerpiece for a weeknight dinner or special occasion.

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 75 minutes
Total Time 1 hour 30 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

75:00

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs), patted dry
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 lemons (1 juiced, 1 sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (or 220°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken in a roasting pan. Rub the garlic-lemon mixture all over the chicken, including under the skin if possible.
  4. Stuff the cavity with lemon slices and onion quarters.
  5. Roast uncovered for 70–80 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) in the thickest part of the thigh.
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve with pan juices.

Chef's Tips

  • For crispier skin, start roasting at a higher temp for 15 minutes, then lower to 375°F (190°C).
  • Use a meat thermometer for perfectly cooked, juicy chicken.
  • Add potatoes or carrots to the roasting pan for a one-pan meal.
  • Basting the chicken with pan juices halfway through boosts flavor.

Serving Suggestions

This roast chicken pairs wonderfully with:

  • Garlic mashed potatoes
  • Roasted root vegetables
  • Steamed green beans or asparagus
  • Crusty bread and a green salad
