Tofu Larb (Vegan Thai Salad)

Tofu Larb is a vibrant, spicy, and tangy Thai-inspired salad made with crumbled tofu, lime juice, fresh herbs, and toasted rice powder. This vegan version offers all the punchy flavors of traditional larb, perfect served in lettuce cups or over jasmine rice.

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 10 minutes
Total Time 25 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

10:00

Ingredients

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, pressed and crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice (fresh)
  • 1 tablespoon vegan fish sauce or extra soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup or sugar
  • 1 shallot, finely sliced
  • 1 Thai chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons toasted rice powder (optional but authentic)
  • Lettuce leaves or cooked rice for serving
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add crumbled tofu and sauté for 5–6 minutes until lightly golden and moisture evaporates.
  2. Stir in soy sauce, lime juice, vegan fish sauce, and maple syrup. Cook for another 2–3 minutes to absorb flavors.
  3. Turn off heat and mix in shallots, chili, mint, cilantro, and toasted rice powder (if using).
  4. Serve warm or at room temperature in lettuce cups or over rice. Garnish with lime wedges.

Chef's Tips

  • To make toasted rice powder, dry-toast uncooked sticky rice in a skillet until golden, then grind to a coarse powder.
  • Use extra firm tofu for best texture. Press well to remove water before crumbling.
  • Adjust spice level to taste by increasing or omitting chili.
  • Can be prepped ahead—flavors deepen after chilling for a few hours.

Serving Suggestions

This tofu larb pairs well with:

  • Butter lettuce or cabbage cups
  • Steamed jasmine or sticky rice
  • Pickled vegetables or Thai cucumber salad
  • Iced Thai tea or lime soda
