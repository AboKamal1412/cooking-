Creamy Mushroom Risotto

This creamy mushroom risotto is a comforting and elegant Italian classic made with Arborio rice, sautéed mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese. Perfect as a main dish or side, it's rich, hearty, and full of savory flavor.

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 30 minutes
Total Time 45 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

30:00

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound mushrooms (cremini or mixed), sliced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 0.5 cup dry white wine
  • 0.5 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the broth in a saucepan and keep warm over low heat.
  2. In a large skillet or pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and mushrooms; sauté until mushrooms are tender and browned.
  4. Stir in the rice and cook for 1-2 minutes until lightly toasted.
  5. Pour in the wine and stir until absorbed.
  6. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly. Allow each addition to be absorbed before adding more. Continue until rice is al dente and creamy (about 20–25 minutes).
  7. Remove from heat and stir in butter, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with parsley and additional Parmesan if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Use warm broth to maintain cooking temperature for better texture.
  • Stir constantly for creamy consistency.
  • Don’t rush—risotto needs gradual broth absorption.
  • Add a splash of cream or mascarpone at the end for extra richness.

Serving Suggestions

This risotto pairs well with:

  • Grilled chicken or shrimp
  • Steamed green beans or roasted Brussels sprouts
  • Crusty garlic bread
  • A glass of Chardonnay or Pinot Noir
