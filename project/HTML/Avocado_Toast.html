Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

Instructions

  1. 1
    Toast sourdough slices until deeply golden and crisp.
  2. 2
    Halve avocado and remove stone. Scoop flesh into a bowl.
  3. 3
    Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper to your preferred texture (chunky or smooth).
  4. 4
    Bring a deep pan of water to a gentle simmer. Add vinegar.
  5. 5
    Create a gentle whirlpool with a spoon and crack one egg into the centre. Poach 3 minutes until white is set. Repeat.
  6. 6
    Remove eggs with a slotted spoon and pat dry briefly.
  7. 7
    Spread avocado generously on toast. Top with poached egg, chili flakes, microgreens, and a drizzle of olive oil.
