Instructions

  1. 1
    Cook pasta in a large pot of heavily salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. 2
    Meanwhile, cook guanciale in a cold pan over medium heat until golden and crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. 3
    Whisk egg yolks, whole egg, Pecorino, Parmesan, and generous black pepper together in a bowl.
  4. 4
    Add hot drained pasta to the guanciale pan (off heat). Toss to coat in the fat.
  5. 5
    Pour egg mixture over pasta and toss vigorously, adding pasta water a splash at a time until creamy and glossy.
  6. 6
    Serve immediately with extra cheese and cracked black pepper.
