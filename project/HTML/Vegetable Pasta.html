Delicious Vegetable Pasta

This colorful and hearty vegetable pasta recipe is loaded with seasonal vegetables, tossed in a light garlic and olive oil sauce, and finished with fresh herbs. It's a healthy, satisfying meal that’s ready in just 30 minutes and perfect for weeknight dinners.

Prep Time 10 minutes
Cook Time 20 minutes
Total Time 30 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

20:00

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta (penne, rotini, or spaghetti)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and yellow squash. Cook for about 5–6 minutes, until slightly tender.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 4–5 minutes until tomatoes soften.
  5. Stir in cooked pasta and reserved pasta water. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  6. Remove from heat, garnish with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan (if using), and serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Use any vegetables you have on hand—broccoli, spinach, mushrooms, and carrots all work well.
  • To make it vegan, skip the cheese or use a dairy-free alternative.
  • Add red pepper flakes for a little kick.
  • Drizzle with balsamic glaze for extra depth of flavor.
  • Double the recipe to have leftovers for lunch the next day.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this vegetable pasta with:

  • Garlic bread or bruschetta
  • Simple green salad with vinaigrette
  • Grilled tofu or a protein of your choice
  • Steamed asparagus or roasted vegetables
  • A glass of Chardonnay or sparkling water with lemon
