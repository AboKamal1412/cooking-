Vietnamese Coffee Smoothie

Vietnamese Coffee Smoothie is a rich, creamy, and energizing treat that blends the bold flavors of Vietnamese coffee with the refreshing texture of a smoothie. This recipe combines strong brewed coffee, sweetened condensed milk, and ice, creating a deliciously indulgent drink perfect for any time of day.

Prep Time 5 minutes
Brewing Time 5-10 minutes
Total Time 10-15 minutes
Servings

Cooking Timer

12:00

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup strong brewed Vietnamese coffee (cooled)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk (adjust to taste)
  • 1 banana (for creaminess)
  • 1 cup ice or frozen coffee cubes for stronger flavor
  • 1/4 cup milk or coconut milk for a lighter texture
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Optional toppings: cocoa powder, crushed peanuts, or a drizzle of condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Brew the Vietnamese coffee and let it cool.
  2. In a blender, combine the cooled coffee, sweetened condensed milk, banana, and ice.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add milk or coconut milk if you want it thinner.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve immediately. Top with optional garnishes.

Chef's Tips

  • Use Authentic Vietnamese Coffee
  • Make Coffee Ice Cubes
  • Adjust Sweetness to Your Taste
  • Try Coconut for a Tropical Twist

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve in a tall chilled glass or a mason jar for a refreshing, café-style presentation.
  • Crushed peanuts or almonds for a crunchy contrast
  • Pair with a light Vietnamese dessert like chè or sesame cookies.
  • Use a wide reusable straw (like stainless steel or bamboo) or serve with a small spoon for thicker versions.
